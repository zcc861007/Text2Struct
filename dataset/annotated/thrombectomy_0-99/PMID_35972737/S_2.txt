Design , Setting , and Participants : This cohort study included patients with large vessel occlusion stroke and prestroke disability ( modified Rankin Scale score , 3 or 4 ) admitted to a single tertiary care center between January 1 , 2006 , and June 30 , 2019 ( controls : 2006 - 2015 ; thrombectomy : 2015 - 2019 ) .
