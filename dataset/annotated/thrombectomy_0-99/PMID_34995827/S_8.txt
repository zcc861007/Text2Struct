In the distal M2 occlusions subgroup , EVT was significantly associated with a higher rate of early neurologic improvement ( adjusted OR : 3.62 0.95 CI : 1.31 - 10.03 ) , NIHSS improvement at discharge ( adjusted mean difference : 5.23 ; 0.95 CI : 2.18 - 8.29 ) , and improved modified Rankin Scale score at 3 months ( adjusted common OR for 1 point improvement : 3.06 ; 0.95 CI : 1.30 to 7.23 ) .
