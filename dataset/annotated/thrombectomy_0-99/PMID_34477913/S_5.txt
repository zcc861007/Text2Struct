Between the patients with anterior and posterior circulation occlusions , the National Institutes of Health Stroke Scale ( NIHSS ) score ( 16 [ 12 - 21 ] vs. 29 [ 19 - 34 ] , respectively , p < 0.001 ) , door-to-puncture time ( 65 [ 45 - 99 ] vs. 99 [ 51 - 121 ] min , respectively , p = 0.018 ) , and mortality ( 22 [ 0.07 ] vs. 8 [ 0.205 ] patients , respectively , p = 0.010 ) were significantly different ; however , favorable outcome was not significantly different .
