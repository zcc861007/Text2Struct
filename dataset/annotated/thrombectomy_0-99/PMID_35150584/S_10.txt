Those with pre-stroke frailty had higher 24 hour National Institutes of Health Stroke Scale ( 12 ( IQR : 8 - 17 ) versus 3 ( IQR : 2 - 13 ) ; P = 0.001 ) ; were less likely to be independent ( mRS 0 - 2 : 0.18 versus 0.61 ; P < 0.001 ) and more likely to die ( 0.47 versus 0.14 ; P < 0.001 ) within 90 days .
