Safety outcomes were similar between groups , with symptomatic intracerebral haemorrhage occurring in two ( 0.01 ) of 146 patients in the direct group and one ( 0.01 ) of 147 patients in the bridging group ( adjusted odds ratio 1.70 , 0.95 CI 0.22 - 13.04 ) and death in 22 ( 0.15 ) of 146 patients in the direct group and 24 ( 0.16 ) of 147 patients in the bridging group ( adjusted odds ratio 0.92 , 0.95 CI 0.46 - 1.84 ) .
