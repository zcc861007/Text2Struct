Key secondary outcomes are : dramatic early favorable response , dichotomized mRS score ( 0 - 2 vs. 3 - 6 and 0 - 4 vs. 5 - 6 ) at 90 days , ordinal ( shift ) mRS analysis at 90 days , infarct volume at 24 h , vessel recanalization at 24 h in both treatment arms , and successful recanalization in the thrombectomy arm according to the modified thrombolysis in cerebral infarction ( mTICI ) classification defined as mTICI 2 b or 3 .
