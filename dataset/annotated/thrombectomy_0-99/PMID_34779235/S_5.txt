A meta-analysis of randomized controlled trial and observational data revealed similar 90-day functional independence ( odds ratio [ OR ] , 1.04 ; 0.95 CI , 0.90 - 1.19 ) , mortality ( OR , 1.03 ; 0.95 CI , 0.78 - 1.36 ) , and successful recanalization ( OR , 0.93 ; 0.95 CI , 0.76 - 1.14 ) for patients treated with dMT or BT .
