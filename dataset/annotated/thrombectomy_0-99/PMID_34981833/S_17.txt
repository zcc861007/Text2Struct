We found no evidence of a difference between USAT and CDT alone for the following evaluated outcomes : amputation rate ( risk ratio ( RR ) 1.14 , 0.95 confidence interval ( CI ) 0.17 to 7.59 ) ; major bleeding ( RR 1.71 , 0.95 CI 0.31 to 9.53 ) ; clinical success ( RR 1.00 , 0.95 CI 0.94 to 1.07 ) ; and adverse effects ( RR 5.69 , 0.95 CI 0.28 to 113.72 ) .
