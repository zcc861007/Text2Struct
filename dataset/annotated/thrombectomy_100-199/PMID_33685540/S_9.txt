Logistic regression analysis identified lower pre-stroke mRS score ( adjusted odds ratio [ aOR ] , 0.52 ; 0.95 CI , 0.36 - 0.76 ) , lower admission NIHSS score ( aOR , 0.90 ; 0.95 CI , 0.83 - 0.97 ) , successful recanalization ( aOR , 3.65 ; 0.95 CI , 1.32 - 10.09 ) , and absence of ICH on follow-up imaging ( aOR , 0.42 ; 0.95 CI , 0.08 - 0.75 ) , to be independent predictors of good outcome .
