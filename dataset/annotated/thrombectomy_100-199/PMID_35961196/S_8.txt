In the multivariable analysis , for patients with poor collaterals , the parameters of higher mean SBP ( 131.7 +/- 12.7 vs. 122.3 +/- 14.2 ; OR , 1.06 ; 0.95 CI , 1.01 - 1.11 ; P 0.022 ) , higher mean MAP ( 91.2 +/- 8.2 vs. 86.1 +/- 6.3 ; OR , 1.13 ; 0.95 CI , 1.03 - 1.23 ; P 0.015 ) and a higher maximum SBP ( 156.3 +/- 13.7 vs. 145.3 +/- 19.1 ; OR , 1.05 ; 0.95 CI , 1.01 - 1.1 ; P 0.019 ) were significantly associated with functional dependence at 3-months .
