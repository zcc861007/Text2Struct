MATERIALS AND METHODS : For this analysis , we included patients presenting to one of two stroke centers between January 2015 and February 2018 with an acute ischemic stroke within 12 h from symptom onset if they had a large vessel occlusion of the anterior circulation documented on magnetic resonance angiography or CTA , baseline MRI pre-EVT with imaging evidence of hypoperfusion , underwent EVT , and had a post-EVT MRI with qualitatively interpretable perfusion-weighted imaging data at 24 h.
