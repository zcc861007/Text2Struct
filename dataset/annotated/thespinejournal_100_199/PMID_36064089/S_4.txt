METHODS : A total of fourty 10-week old Sprague-Dawley rats were used in this study and assigned to three groups as follow : disc curettage only ( control group , n = 10 ) , disc curettage + HAp/Col transplant ( H group , n = 10 ) , and disc curettage + HAp/Col + PRP transplant ( H+P group , n = 10 ) .
