Multivariate logistic regression analysis showed significant associations between LBP and diabetes ( OR 2.43 ; 0.95 CI 1.07 - 5.53 ) , buttock and leg numbness on the NRS ( OR 1.34 ; 0.95 CI 1.17 - 1.52 ) , general health on the SF-36 ( OR 0.97 ; 0.95 CI 0.95 - 0.99 ) , and the presence of erosive endplate defects ( OR 3.04 ; 0.95 CI 1.51 - 6.11 ) ( all p < .05 ) .
