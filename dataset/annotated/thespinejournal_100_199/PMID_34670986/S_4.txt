MATERIALS AND METHODS : A national database was queried for patients undergoing lumbar fusion and divided into 4 groups : ( 1 ) those who underwent LSF without THA ( No THA ) ; ( 2 ) those who underwent THA at least 2 years before LSF ( > 2 Prior THA ) ; ( 3 ) those who underwent THA in the 2 years before LSF ( 0 - 2 Prior THA ) ; and ( 4 ) those who underwent THA after LSF ( THA After ) .
