The clinical laboratory indexes of the two groups before and after the operation were compared : C-reactive protein ( CRP ) , erythrocyte sedimentation rate ( ESR ) , creatine phosphokinase ( CPK ) , interleukin-6 ( IL-6 ) , tumor necrosis factor-alpha ( TNF-alpha ) , as well as the incidence of complications , such as dural tear , nerve root injury and infection .
