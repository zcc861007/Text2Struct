STUDY DESIGN/SETTING : Single center retrospective cohort PATIENT SAMPLE : Patients undergoing posterior cervical or lumbar spinal fusion between January 2000 and October 2020 OUTCOME MEASURES : Postoperative surgical site infection status METHODS : Patients were grouped based on our institutionally derived dosing adequacy standards , 1 g for < 60 kg , 2 g for 60 to 120 kg , and 3 g for > 120 kg .
